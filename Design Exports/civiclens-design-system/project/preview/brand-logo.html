Flat geometric direction. The glyph is a circular lens (2.5px navy stroke) with a short handle stub at the 4–5 o'clock position. Inside, a flat US flag — navy canton with 9 white solid 5-point stars (3×3 grid), 5 alternating stripes in white and muted red #8a2929 — clipped by the lens edge. No gradients, no glass illusion, no painted softness; one thin 14% diagonal sheen reads as "lens" without becoming illustration. Stars stylize for legibility — they collapse to a textured block at 16px, which is intended. Wordmark is Space Grotesk 600 with letter-spacing: -0.025em. Green is intentionally absent — accent green stays reserved for in-app interaction states.
